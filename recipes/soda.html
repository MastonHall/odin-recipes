Dellicious Homemade Orange Soda

This recipe will make a fantastic summer treat for you and your friends to enjoy!

What we will need

Syrup

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Soda

  • 11/2 cups orange juice
  • 11/2 cups sparkling water

Steps to make

  1. Combine the water, maple syrup, honey, and vanilla extract in a small saucepan over medium heat and stir constantly 2-3 minutes, until well-mixed and somewhat thickened.
  2. Remove from heat and chill thoroughly.
  3. Divide the orange juice and sparkling water evenly between four ice-filled 8-ounce glasses, add syrup to taste, and stir well before serving.
